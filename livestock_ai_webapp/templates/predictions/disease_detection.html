About Lumpy Skin Disease

Lumpy skin disease is a viral disease that affects cattle, causing skin lesions, fever, and reduced milk production. Early detection is crucial for treatment and prevention.

Symptoms:
  • • Raised skin nodules
  • • Fever and lethargy
  • • Reduced appetite
  • • Drop in milk production
Prevention:
  • • Regular health monitoring
  • • Proper vaccination
  • • Biosecurity measures
  • • Early detection systems
